Hello Earth

Interactive Children's Book

Background

Creating own story and characters

Tools Used: InDesign | Photoshop | Pencil & Paper

For Indesign final project, we were given a choice of making a educational magazine and my own children's book. There was a story that I wanted to tell to children since the emergence of digital era so I chose to make my own children's book. The story is about an alien discovering in awe about how people interact.

Process

Step 1 - Storyboards

After finalizing the story in my head, I started to draw storyboards. One of the challenges was getting the perspective and angle of the shot in a way I wanted. I tried to keep it as rough and simple as possible while solidifying where things should be located.

Step 2 - Painting

After scanning all of the storyboards, I imported each page onto Photshop and started painting environments, people, and things that should be interacted separately in InDesign at a later stage in a different layer. I utilized custom brushes and blending mode to achieve unique atmosphere around my book.

Step 3 - Interaction

I imported separate objects in two formats (JPG and PNG) and organized them in different groups and layers in InDesign. From there, I created interactions page by page using state objects, built-in animation, and CSS animation. I also optimized all the images in-between using Color Quantizer for a ligther file size.

Reflections

Result

The outcome of this project was successful that it resulted in a perfect score. The overall look turned out better than I expected even though it was my first attempt to fully draw and paint the environment.

What I learned

I learned what I can do with animation features in InDesign in depth as well as becoming comfortable with state objects, which was challenging to learn in the beginning. I also realized what it takes to create a single children's book as this took over a month to finish it.

Next Steps

Based on how much efforts and time I put in, it was very satisfying to see a finished version. Areas such as Lighting, dialogue, perspectives can be improved. I will keep practicing from now on.
