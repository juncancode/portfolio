Nature's Path Promo Video

Motion Graphics

Background

Storytelling through motion graphics

Tools Used: Adobe After Effects | Pencil & Papers

For the final project for visual effects class, we were asked to create a minute-video that showcases various animation techniques. I chose to make a promotion video for my favourite cereal brand, Nature's Path. I mainly utilized Trim Path, Alpha Matt, and Effects.

Process

Step 1 - Planning

Storyboarding each scene was an essential part of the process. More details I lay out on papers, it gave me much clear ideas on what tools I should utilize.
I wrote the orders of animation by numbering them and used different colors to discern elements.

Step 2 - Tweaking the timing

After roughly blocking the overall animation. I worked on the adjusting timing and position of each element in order to make it as close as possible to the original plan. Graph Editor and Keyframes Assistant were used to fix the timing.

Step 3 - Combining scenes

I applied the same process to each scene and combined layers in each scene by precomposing and put them in an order. Parenting and naming layers helped me organize the layers efficiently.

Reflections

Result

Nothing feels more satisfied than watching a finished animation. This project let me practice the main tools of After Effects.

What I learned

Through trials and errors, I learned how to best uitlize Alpha and Luma Matt for masking. Transitioning from one scene to another by using different techniques gave me many possibilities to be creative.

Next Steps

I will create an enhanced version which includes sound effects and more complex masking techniques.
