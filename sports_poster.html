Baseball Poster

Photo Editing

Background

Creating Quality Poster

Tools Used: Photoshop | Illustrator

Creating a high-quality poster was a final project for the advanced Photoshop class. As an avid baseball fan, I chose to do a baseball poster that presents the Boston Red Sox, who won the World Series in 2018.

We were required to use all the technquies we learned throughout the course such as, Vector Smart Objects, Layer Styles, Blending Mode, Rasterized Text, and Custom Brushes.

Process

Step 1 - Researching

I have learned that by doing a thorough research, the result can make a difference. I looked at existing posters and sketched the composition prior to downloading the images that meet my needs. The logo and pennant were created in Illustrator and imported in Photoshop to be used with Layer Styles.

Step 2 - Feedback

After laying out the basic the composition and effects, I seeked constructive feedback from both my classmates and instructor. The trophy surrounded by players made it look like it was fighting for an attention and the logo was not visible enough. The painting mask effect was a bit overdone as well.

Step 3

I started to make changes based on the feedback I received and improved areas such as color correcting players uniforms, aligning players symmetrically, and hiding advertisments for impartial reasons. Finally, custom-made confetti and halftone texture were added.

Reflections

Result

The outcome of the project was satisfying as I made significant changes through feedback and a number of retouches. The color correction and the vibrance from the logo can be polished more.

What I learned

I realized the importance of a good composition and color breaks as they contribute greatly to overall feels. My ability to spot areas that can be improved grew as a designer and I became more opened to critiques. Because even people with no design background can spot a critical point that I do not notice.

Next Steps

The contrast can be enhanced around the logo by painting a darker color. Additional effects can also be used to increase the overall intensity.
